About me

I am a part-time AI Research Assistant at the University of Oklahoma and a Lecturer in the Department of AI and Data Science at FAST University, Lahore. I earned my MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of California, Irvine (UCI) in 2023 with a GPA of 3.95, following my BS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Lahore, where I graduated with a GPA of 3.04.

With over three years of research experience through remote assistantships at Purdue University, the University of Oklahoma, UC Irvine, and Integrated Medical Sensors (IMS), California, I am committed to pursuing a PhD at the intersection of data science, machine learning, and AI , advancing as both a learner and innovator to shape impactful contributions in the field.
