About me

I earned my MSc in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of California, Irvine (UCI) with a GPA of 3.95 (2023), following my BSc in Electrical Engineering with a GPA of 3.04 (2019) from the University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Lahore. Currently, I am working as a remote AI Research Scientist at a California-based healthcare technology company, Integrated Medical Sensors. I am also serving as a Lecturer in the Department of AI and Data Science at FAST National University, Lahore.

With over three years of AI research experience through remote assistantships at Purdue University, the University of Oklahoma, UC Irvine, and Integrated Medical Sensors (IMS), California, I am dedicated to pursuing a PhD at the intersection of data science, machine learning, and AI, advancing as both a researcher and innovator to deliver impactful contributions to this field.
