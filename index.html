About me

I am a part-time CS Education Research Intern at both Purdue University and the University of Oklahoma. In addition, I serve as a Lecturer at the Department of AI and Data Science at FAST School of Computing in Lahore, Pakistan. I hold a Master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of California, Irvine (UCI), graduating in 2023 with a GPA of 3.954. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore, Pakistan, with a GPA of 3.04, in 2019. With over three years of hands-on research experience and a strong passion for teaching, I aim to pursue CS Education research through a PhD to increase diversity, equity, and inclusion in CS Education.
